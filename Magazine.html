Magazine
Sthapati is the annual magazine published by the students of the department, incorporating their hard work and creativity under able guidance and help from the esteemed faculty members. It is a compilation of articles, interviews, research papers, compositions and updates from the field of architecture all over the globe. Eminent professors contribute their expertise in architecture research field and the alumni pass their practical experiences from their initial days of learning and work, thus making it an ideal digest of architectural information of refined taste.
